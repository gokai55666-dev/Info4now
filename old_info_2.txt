📋 1. Core Prompt Structure (Formula)

Standard Five‑Element Formula

Implementation:
Subject → Action → Scene → Camera → Style/Atmosphere

Example (English – works well):

A young woman in a white dress twirls gracefully in a sun‑drenched forest clearing. Medium wide shot. Golden hour lighting, cinematic quality, small dynamic.

Example (Chinese – excellent, native tuning):

一个穿着白色连衣裙的年轻女孩在一片阳光普照的森林空地上优雅地旋转。中景广角镜头。黄金时刻光线，电影质感，小动态。

Best language: both work similarly, though Chinese often yields more accurate interpretation of subtle scene details.
Success rate: ≥ 95% for basic coherent videos.

👎 4. Negative Prompts (Standard format for Vidu Pro)

Valid prefix rule

Must use: no, not, without, un‑, non‑
Invalid: avoid, exclude, don't (not recognized by CLIP encoder)

Length limit

Keep under 20 English tokens (or equivalent Chinese characters)

Standard negative prompt list (Pro – dedicated field)

Weighted syntax (optional):

Best language: Chinese appears to be given higher priority by the model.
Success rate: With correctly formatted negatives inside the Pro dedicated field, ~85–95% improvement in avoiding listed flaws.

🌐 5. Language Support & Prompt Language Choice

Officially supported: More than 200 languages.
In practice for best results:

· Chinese (native-level understanding) – ideal for motion dynamics, scene logic, and subtle prompts.
· English (very good) – works well for style, quality tags (4K, cinematic), and audio generation.
· Other languages (risky): Japanese may trigger a 400 error; French may cause semantic deviation.

Best practice: Mix both – use Chinese for action, motion, and negative prompts; use English for style, quality, and audio cues.
Success rate: Chinese‑only or bilingual prompts achieve ~90% intent accuracy; other languages → ≤60% risk of errors.

🎤 7. Audio Generation (Vidu Q3 / Pro only)

Dialogue (script format)

Use speaker label + tone tag + pacing + performance cue.

English example (recommended):

WOMAN (whispering, urgent): "We have to leave now." (fast pacing, anxious tone).

Chinese example (also effective):

女人（低声，急促地说）："我们得马上离开。"（快节奏，焦虑的语气）。

Best language: English → cleaner lip‑sync and tone control.
Success rate: ~85% for simple 1‑2 line dialogue; ~70% for multi‑character.

Ambient sound / SFX

Examples: birds chirping, footsteps on gravel, faint whoosh, room tone.
Best language: English.
Success rate: ~75–85%.

BGM (background music)

Examples: ambient piano, melancholic, upbeat electronic.
Best language: English.
Success rate: ~70%.
